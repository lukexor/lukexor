About

Hi. My name is Luke. I reside in Portland, OR working out of a menagerie of coffee shops and pie establishments. I've been tinkering with code since high school (mostly perl at the time) when I first learned what HTML was. Oh, what a journey it has been.

I'm still developing mostly in perl these days, but I also dabble in Python and Objective-C in my spare time. Over the years I've developed a borderline addiction to problem solving with many late nights, refusing to go to bed until I was satisfied I had reached an acceptable stopping point.

I'm constantly thirsting for new things to learn and more efficient ways to solve problems. I'm excited about emerging technologies and what else I can sink my teeth into. I post articles here to share ideas and spur discussions about code and technology.
